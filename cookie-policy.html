Cookie Policy

What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small data files that are placed on your device when you visit a website. Cookies are widely used to make websites work, or to work more efficiently, as well as to provide reporting information and personalised content.

Why We Use Cookies

We use cookies for several reasons. Some cookies are required for technical reasons in order for our website to operate. Others help us understand how visitors interact with our site, improve user experience, and support our marketing efforts.

Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for site functionality and security.
  • Performance Cookies: Help us analyse site usage and improve performance (e.g. Hotjar, Google Analytics).
  • Functional Cookies: Enable enhanced functionality and personalisation.
  • Marketing Cookies: Track browsing habits to deliver relevant ads and marketing (e.g. HubSpot).

Managing Cookie Preferences

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can set or amend your cookie preferences using your browser settings or our cookie consent tool. Please note that disabling certain cookies may impact the functionality of our website.

Third-Party Cookies

Some cookies on our website are served by third parties, including analytics providers and marketing partners. These third-party services may collect data about your online activities over time and across different websites.
